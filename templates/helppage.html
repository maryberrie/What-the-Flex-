Help/Tutorial

Welcome to the game and thanks for playing!

This is a simple text RPG game.

When you see a comment box, feel free to express whatever you would like.
Key phrases may open up something new that could be of help to you in the game of life, your life.
Try "blood, sweat, tears". It may clean up some hardships in your life.
Try "danger". It may magically make all your worries disappear.
Try "butterfly". It may help you get out of an abusive situation.
Try "not today". It may convince you that today is not the day to give up.
Try "BTS". Maybe you'll go behind the scenes?

In this game, the decision is yours. Your choices will dictate your survival. Good luck! And have fun!
