Damsel in Distress

As you and Poro walk the way the old woman mentioned, you see a shining waterfall through a small parting in the leaves on the trees. The two of you go a little closer to the shiny phenomenon and see that there is even a boat floating next to the waterfall as if it was meant to bring people into the cave behind the waterfall. The cave! It must be the magical cave. Despite this, you still have some doubts about this decision because what if there is a monster inside the cave?
