Damsel in Distress

As you touch the stone, you feel a boost of confidence and a rush of adrenaline in your body. Suddenly you feel light and like you can conquer anything in the world. Because of this, your rowing becomes faster, and you reach the end of the cave quickly...only to see that there is an obstacle in the way: a dragon.

You go into panic-mode for a few seconds but when you look down, you see the shiny stone clutched in your hands. You muster up all your courage. The shiny stone suddenly gives you magical powers, and jumping off the boat, you charge across the water and approach the dragon. You jump high enough to be face to face to the dragon and you...
