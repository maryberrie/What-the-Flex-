Damsel in Distress

You knock on the old wooden door on the cabin. After a long while, an old woman cracks a door open and peeks out. You quickly stutter that you are lost and do not know the way out of the forest. Fortunately, she is a kind old woman and directs you to two possible pathes to go. One is the Magical Cave, which can take you directly out of the Enchanted Forest. The other is a field on the other side of the forest that has flowers with a potion that can transport you out of the Enchanted Forest.
